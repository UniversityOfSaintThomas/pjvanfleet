NoiseEstimate

NoiseEstimate[data]
takes a vector or matrix and a wavelet filter and returns an estimate of the noise.

DetailsDetails

  • Hampel's noise estimate of the data is the median absolute deviation of the data divided by 0.6745:
    = MAD[data]/0.6745
