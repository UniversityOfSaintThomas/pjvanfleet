IntegerMap  (1)IntegerMap  (1)

The LeGall wavelet transform employs the lifting scheme to perform the computations and can, by setting IntegerMap to True, map integer-valued input to integer-valued output. The integer-valued mapping is invertible - see LeftInverseLWT.
