InverseFBIPacketTransform

InverseFBIPacketTransform[wt]
computes the inverse wavelet packet transform of the inpt matrix or list of three matrices.

DetailsDetails

  • InverseFBIPacketTransform[data] is equivalent to InverseWPT[ FBITree[]},CDF97[],NumIterations->5,CompositeTree->True,Computation->Numerical,Boundary->True]
  • The input data must be a matrix or a list of three matrices of equal dimension.
