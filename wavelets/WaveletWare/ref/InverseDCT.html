InverseDCT

InverseDCT[a]
computes the inverse discrete cosine transform (II) of an input vector or matrix.

DetailsDetails

  • The function uses the Mathematica function FourierDCT[a,3] (the inverse of FourierDCT[,2]) to compute the inverse discrete cosine transform of a.
  • For vector input, all but the first element of the inverse discrete cosine transform is scaled by . For matrix input, the elements in the first row and column of the inverse discrete cosine trasnfrorm (except the 1,1 element) are scaled ; elements in all other rows and columns are scaled by .
