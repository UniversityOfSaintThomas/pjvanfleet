nCE


takes a cumulative energy vector v and a value pct ∈ [0,1] and returns an index k that represents the number of values in v that are less than or equal to pct.

DetailsDetails

  • nCE appears in DiscreteWavelets packages and is replaced by PercentCE in the WaveletWare package.
  • nCE returns the minimum number of the largest components (in modulus) in v that constitute at most 100*pct% of the energy in v.
