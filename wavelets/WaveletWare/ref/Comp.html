Comp


takes a vector or matrix a and a positive integer k and returns a vector or matrix where the largest k (absolute) values plus any redundancies are preserved and the remaining values are converted to 0.

DetailsDetails

  • Comp appears in DiscreteWavelets packages and is replaced by QuantizeCE in the WaveletWare package.
