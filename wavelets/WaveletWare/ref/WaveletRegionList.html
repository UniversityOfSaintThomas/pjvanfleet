WaveletRegionList

WaveletRegionList[tree]
takes a wavelet packet tree or a list of three trees (for a color image) and returns a list of n-tuples (pairs, triples, 4-tuples for a vector, matrix, or list of three matrices, respectively) that correspond to the regions represented by the tree.
